Who We Are

Built on Four Principles

Sigma Lambda Beta International Fraternity, Inc. was founded in 1986. The Delta Zeta Chapter was established at Elmhurst University on April 28, 2023 — making history as the university's first Latino-based fraternity.

📋 501(c)(7) Organization Sigma Lambda Beta International Fraternity, Inc. operates as a federally recognized 501(c)(7) nonprofit social organization. Contributions support fraternity programming, community events, and student development.
  • 📚
    Scholarship
    Encouraging academic excellence through study sessions, workshops, and peer accountability among members.
  • 🤝
    Brotherhood
    Fostering strong, lasting relationships built on trust, mutual respect, and shared values.
  • 🌱
    Community Service
    Actively giving back through volunteer events, campus collaborations, and local outreach initiatives.
  • 🎭
    Cultural Awareness
    Celebrating Latino heritage and fostering inclusion across the diverse Elmhurst University community.

Our Story

From Interest Group
to Chapter

November 2020
Alumni Board Founded
Elmhurst University SLB alumni brothers establish a board to mentor aspiring members and lay the groundwork for a campus chapter.
Spring 2021
EU Men in Purple Interest Group Forms
Rene Sandoval connects with alumni at the EU Interest Fair, sparking the formation of a group committed to bringing SLB to Elmhurst — the first of its kind on campus.
Fall 2021 – Fall 2022
Growth & Expansion
Kevin Johnson, Brian Hernandez, and Max Ojeda join the interest group, solidifying a core team dedicated to community service, leadership, and cultural awareness.
April 28, 2023
Delta Zeta Chapter Officially Established
After two years of work, Sigma Lambda Beta is officially founded at Elmhurst University — the first Latino-based fraternity in the university's history. Founding line: L.S. V.4.D.E.
2023 – Present
Continued Growth
Seven additional members welcomed. 22+ events hosted. 350+ community members served. Chapter petition for official status submitted December 2024.
